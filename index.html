Carbon in Context

How much CO2 is emitted by producing a burger, or by taking a transatlantic flight, or by a country each year?

The media throws around kilograms, tonnes and gigatonnes, but it’s hard to understand how they compare. So here’s a visualization that puts them in context.

By Duncan Geere, February 2020

Data from multiple sources, including Our World in Data, The Guardian and the Exponential Climate Action Roadmap. See the datasheet for full information.

Made with love and D3.js. I call this type of visualization a "logpile chart", find out more in this short essay, or get the source code on Github.
